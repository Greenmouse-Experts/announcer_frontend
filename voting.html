Voting Management

Home

/ Voting Management

Election

We provide a platform to set up Election and for voters to cast their votes.  When the Election Starts, each of the voters will receive an email with a secret, single use voting link, the voters clicked on the link to be immediately taken to the voting page to cast vote for the aspirant. Voters can only vote once and voting choices remain anonymous, each ballot has one, secure voting key and the voting is auditable, verifiable and can be independently observed.

This is specially made for

  • Unions

  • Educations

  • Associations

  • Cooperatives

  • Corporate and Industry

Voting Contest

We also provide a platform to set up Vote for Contest, voters can vote as many as possible for their favourite to win a contest.  Invite your audience to participate by uploading their Photos and set up a public online voting. You can as well provide the participating entries and invite the public to vote for winners to emerge. Our comprehensive anti-fraud system ensures secured and reliable voting.

This is specially made for

  • Musical Contest

  • Video Contest

  • Photo Contest

  • Writing Contest

  • Brand Contest

  • And other Contest
