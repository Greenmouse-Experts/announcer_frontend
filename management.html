ID Card Management

While ID Cards are a necessity, paying huge to hire a Graphic designer need not be, with our Digital ID Card Management, you can easily create Unique Digital ID Card for yourself, Business, staff members, Association members, Club members etc. No design experience is needed to use this solution. Choose from any of our ready-made templates then customize to your desire. You can experiment with different designs until you create one that is right.

Once you have finished your ID Card design, you can easily download and print your ID Card. Our Digital ID Card Provides a Unique QR Code

Features

  • Insert Logo

  • Insert Signature

  • DIY Digital ID Card Solution

  • Share Digital ID Cards via Email and Social Media Platforms

  • Digital ID Card includes a Barcode which are scannable with Scanners.

  • Create design that suite to the requirement of your Organization or Business

  • User can take their own ID photo in the App or upload from their Photo gallery

  • Choose from our Ready-made Designed Templates and print ID Cards directly from your Portal

Types Of ID Card

  • Alumni ID Card : Digital ID Card issued by Colleges and Universities

  • Student ID Card  :   Digital ID Card issued by Schools to the Students          

  • Membership ID Card: Digital ID Card issued by associations and Clubs

  • Religion ID Card :   Digital ID Card issued to Religion Members and Units

  • Employee ID Card : Digital ID Card issued by Organizations and Businesses

  • Resident ID Card: Digital ID Card issued by Streets, Town and City authorities    

  • Union ID Card :  Digital ID Card issued by trade and labour union associations

  • Professional ID Card  : Digital ID Card issued by licensing and Certification authorities
