Frequently Asked Questions

What are the services provided by Announcers?

  • ID card Management: Choose from our pre-designed Digital ID card templates and create for yourself or organization with a unique QR Code

  • Certificate Management: Choose from pre-designed Digital Certificate Templates and create for your next Event, Award, and Certificate of Recognition etc. with unique QR Code

  • Business Menu: Discover how brands and institutions are using QR Codes to engage potential customers. This service is for all Businesses and service providers

  • Event Ticket Sales: As an event organizer, we provide you with Ticket sales solution that makes you manage your event, monitor ticket sales and get real-time data. Our Ticket sales solution comes with QR Code that can be scanned for authenticity.

  • Free Event Access Card: We provide Access Card for all events type (Paid or Free). Seminar, Wedding, Funeral, Birthday, etc.

  • Automated Invoice: Take advantage of our beautifully designed invoice template features by personalizing your bills with your business logo and signature, sending your invoices directly to your customers through email or WhatsApp. This is huge improvement from when you wrote invoice by hand.

  • Voting Management: We provide a platform to drive your voting contest and also Online Election for Associations, Schools etc.

How does your platform facilitate digital event management?

  • Create Event: Provide key details like Event name, Venue, Date, Banner and other basic information

  • Setup Event Page: Setup your custom event registration page; this is where guests can view your event details, register and purchase Tickets for your event. You will receive payments directly into your Account.

  • Design Tickets: Choose from a range of pre-designed templates; add background, image, color, logo and more. You can create different Ticket designs/ Prices for your event

  • Share Event link: Directly share the Event link on social media for publicity

What do you provide on ID card and Certificate Management?

  • You can use our Digital ID card management for creating your Digital Identity card.

  • Digital identity is an identity created on digital ledger that can be used by user to represent themselves in digital world using established identities from authorized organization.

  • You can also use our Digital Certificate management for creating Digital Certificate for recognition, skill acquired, event participation, knowledge or experience gained, Awards.

  • Digital ID card and Digital Certificate are unique with QR code which is globally available

  • Directly share the Digital ID card or Certificate via email or  WhatsApp. You can also export and print

How do I promote my event on your website?

  • Simply login into your account, create the event and wait for approval from the admin, after the approval of your event, click on promote event on your dashboard to promote the event on Announcer website.

How do I pay for service I want on your website?

  • Simply fund your wallet using our online payment solution, your wallet will be instantly funded once the payment is successful. The money in your Deposit wallet can be used for all the service on our website except Ticket sales and Voting contest that requires direct payment into Organizer wallet.

What are my roles as an agent?

  • You are to onboard active users using your referral link and train the users on how to effectively use all the services, agents must minimum 100 active users within 90days of becoming an agent.

  • Agent earns 20% instant commission of the Cash deposit made by users under the agent. The more your users deposit into their wallets to use any of our services, the more you earn.

How do I withdraw my Commission?

  • Simply click on Withdrawal on your Agent dashboard and make your withdrawal. Kindly note that payments are processed 8pm – 10pm (Monday – Friday)

Can I withdraw fund in my Deposit Account?

  • Absolutely No! Fund in your deposit account can only be used for purchase of any of the services on the website; only fund in Commission Wallet can be withdrawn.
